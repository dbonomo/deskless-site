Frequently asked

Do I need a tech team?

No. We work with the tools you already have and handle the technical implementation ourselves.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

Professional services firms, marketing agencies, operations teams, and any mid-market organization managing distributed or frontline workforces. The fit is best for teams between 10 and 500 people.

How is this different from hiring a consultant to write a strategy doc?

We build things. The deliverable is a working AI workflow and a trained team, not a report.

What does the AI Readiness Assessment actually include?

Stakeholder interviews, workflow mapping, tool audit, risk review, and a prioritized roadmap. Delivered in 2 weeks. Costs $3,500. If we find there's nothing worth building, we tell you that and refund the unused portion.

Can you work with our existing tools (Slack, HubSpot, Notion, etc.)?

Yes. We start from what you have.

Why "Deskless"?

Most AI consulting is built around knowledge workers in front of screens. A lot of the hardest, most valuable work in the world happens somewhere else — in the field, on a job site, in a distribution center. That's who we build for.
